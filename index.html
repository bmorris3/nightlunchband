We Are Night Lunch

Night Lunch is an alternative rock band formed in Seattle, Washington. The four piece group consists of vocalist Natalie Nicole, guitarist Brett Morris, bassist Spencer Wallace, and dummer Trevor Zaylen. They released their first studio EP “Am I Falling” in April 2019.
