We Are Night Lunch

Night Lunch is an alternative rock outfit formed in Seattle, Washington. The four piece group consists of vocalist Natalie Nicole, guitarist Brett Morris, bassist Spencer Wallace, and dummer Trevor Zaylen. They released their first demo “Night Lunch Cometh” in May 2017 and are currently in the studio working on their first EP “Sunday Brunch with Night Lunch” due out in Spring 2018.
